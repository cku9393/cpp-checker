- gate meanings:
  - PASS
    - direct compared/eligible/completed targets met
    - semantic_disagreement_count=0
    - fallback_count=0
    - semantic_shift_count=0
  - PROXY_PASS
    - direct family remains under-generated
    - proxy compare family reaches PASS
    - lineage sample target is met
    - same_semantic_class_count and same_final_state_count stay perfect across lineage samples
    - followup_pattern_preserved_count and structure_only_enrichment_count stay perfect across lineage samples
  - NON_APPLICABLE
    - audited states meet applicability target
    - compare_relevance and split_ready_relevance stay below configured maxima
    - dominant ineligible reason stays no_split_ready at or above configured confidence
  - FAIL
    - semantic disagreement, fallback, semantic shift, or evidence shortfall
